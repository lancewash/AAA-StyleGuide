i Sidebar Tool

Four variants are available for Sidebar Tools. Each has a unique functionality. Only one may be used per page.

  • A. Login - Used on top-level pages to drive members to login.
  • B. Quicklinks - Used highlight important related links to content or actions. This should be the default if other tools aren’t needed.
  • C. Contact - Used to provide specific contact information. Variants: Locator, Download, Phone Number, Live Chat
  • D. Call to Action - Used in conjunction with content to highlight a task or initiative. Variants: 1-3 Call to Action buttons possible
