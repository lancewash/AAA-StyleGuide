Sitecore Instructions - Page Editor/Adding Content to a Page (template)

  1. Page Editor/Adding Content to a Page (template)

    • Open in Experience Editor
    • Click in Hero placeholder, click on More, select Edit Component Properties
    • In Control Properties window that opens, click on Browse to choose content item from correct data folder, adjust hero size if needed in Parameters.
    • Repeat for other sections/types of data
